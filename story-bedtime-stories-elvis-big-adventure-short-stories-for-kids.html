LET’S DISCUSS THE STORIES ~ IDEAS FOR TALKING WITH KIDS

Friendship

1. What kind of things you think makes people (or animals!) friends?

2. In this story. what do you think shows Elvis that the animals who join him are his friends?

Imagination

1. What do you think they will do together next, now they are friends?
